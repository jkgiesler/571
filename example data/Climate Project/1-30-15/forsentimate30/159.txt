What if you had an idea that you believed could change the world? How would you convince people that your idea could become a reality? A Canadian inventor named Louis Michaud has spent decades building a machine—a tornado machine—that he thinks could solve the world's energy problems. According to Michaud, his " Atmospheric Vortex Engine " may someday generate mile-high columns of warm air, heated by the sun or waste heat from power plants, which could turn turbines and produce power.
