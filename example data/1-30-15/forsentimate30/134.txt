✔ Consistently Tweeting engaging content is hard work for one individual. Grouptweet allows you to enlist multiple contributors for help.

✔ Stop sharing your Twitter password! Allow any number of contributors to Tweet from your Twitter account safely and securely.

✔ Track contributor participation and quantify contributor performance! GroupTweet allows you to track who Tweeted what and quickly see which contributors Tweet the best content via our "contributor analytics" feature.

✔ No need to convince contributors to learn a new dashboard. Contributors simply either send a Tweet or Direct Message from whichever Twitter client each of them prefers.

✔ Additionally, GroupTweet can enable private group messaging via Twitter. The only way possible to send group Direct Messages.
