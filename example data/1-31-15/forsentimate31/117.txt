This song, written to the tune of Paul Simon's "50 Ways to Leave Your Lover," teaches about carbon reduction and stabilization wedges. The song highlights ideas developed by Stephen Pacala and Robert Socolow at Princeton's Carbon Mitigation Institute. Major concepts covered include atmospheric carbon emission levels, the carbon stabilization triangle, carbon wedges, and carbon reduction strategies. Lyrics are available are the Carbon Mitigation Initiative's website, and at www.teacherspayteachers.com
