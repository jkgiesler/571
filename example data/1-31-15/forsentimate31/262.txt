Don’t wreck deel, US warns, as climate talks jam

© AFP/File Eitun AbramovichThousans participate n t' Folk’s Climate March un t' sidelines ot' UN COP20 an CMP10 climate change conferences bein helt n Lima un Decemb'r 10, 2014Lima (AFP) – T' Unitid States urget developyun countries Thirsdee ta ease objectyuns ta a worl deel un climate change as deedlines loomet at a 12-day UN meetin n Lima.

T' talks, meent ta pave t' way ta a lanmark pact n Paris nex year, air skeduled ta conclude un Frydee, but delegates repertid deedlock an a souryun mood as t' final day neeret.

N a speech touchin un one ot' therniest issues, US Secretree oState John Kerry callt un developyun natyuns ta unnerstan thay too had ta curb carbon emissyuns eve if'n thay felt it wuz unfair.

“I knoe t' discussyuns a'ken be tense an decisyuns air difficult an I knoe hoe onry sum folk air about t' predicament thay’ve bee put n by big natyuns at have benefitid frum industrializashun fer a long period otime,” Kerry sed.

But, “we have ta rememb'r at today mer thun half oglobal emissyuns — mer thun half — air a'kummin frum developyun natyuns. So it is imperatif at thay ack too.”If'n t' negotiatyuns fail, “future generatyuns will judge air effurt nairy jes as a policy failure but as a massif collectif meral failure ohisteric consekwance,” he warnet.

T' envisyunet pact would brang all t' worl’s natyuns into a sangle ferum fer cuttin heat-trappyun greenhouse gases blamet fer damagyun Earth’s climate systems.

Ta be seelet n Decemb'r 2015 an ent'r into ferce by 2020, it must ensure global warmin duz nairy exceet acoupla degrees Celsius (3.6 degrees Fahrenheit) ov'r pre-industrial levels.

T' Lima roun cummenced un Decemb'r 1 un a hopefil note, a'followin promises by China, t' Unitid States an t' Yuropeen Union — t' worl’s three biggest carbon polluters — ta tackle thar emissyuns.

Money, too, cummenced ta floe into t' Gree Climate Fund, t' mane vehicle fer channeleeun at leest $100 billyun (80 billyun euros) n annual aid, promissd by rich countries ta climate-vulnerabull natyuns by 2020.

But un Thirsdee t' negotiatyuns groun un wit few signs ogenuine herse-tradyun.

UN Secretree Genrull Bun Ki-moon urget ministers ta cummence “real, seriyus negotiatyuns.”

“We have bee a'talkin ov'r t' las acoupla decades,” sed Bun. “We don’t have a moment ta lose.”



– Logjams –



Delegates pointid ta acoupla logjams.

One is a draft blueprint fer guidyun negotiatyuns up ta t' Decemb'r 2015 finale — a document which has balloonet as countries filet mer objectyuns an suggestyuns.

“We have see t' laggards throwin n langwage oall kinds into t' negotiatyun document,” Tony de Brum, forn minster ot' Marshull Islans, tole journalists.

T' uther is a fermat fer stanardizyun informayshun by which countries will make carbon-cuttin pledges at t' heert ot' 2015 accerd.

Developyun countries demanet pledges incerporate nairy jes acshun un reducyun carbon emissyuns, but finanshul hep an adaptashun aid ta shere up climate defenses.

T' sparryun touchd un a yeers-old sorce ofricshun — hoe ta appershun responsibilty tween rich an developyun natyuns fer rampyun up carbon acshun.

Knowed as differantiashun, it is rootid n a division oresponsibilitees enshrinet n t' Unitid Natyuns Framewerk Convenshun un Climate Change at its birth 22 yeers ago.

At t' time, UN members wuz split into advancet ekunomies, t' furst ta burn fossil fuels ta pow'r thar prosperitee, an developyun countries, which — until the — bore lil blame fer carbon pollushun.

Yurop, t' Unitid States an uther advancet ekunomies accept at sum form odifferantiashun remuns valid today.

But thay argue it is senseless ta shape a post-2020 deel un t' basis ot' worl as it wuz n 1992.

Veraciyus burners ocoal, developyun countries noe account fer mer thun half oglobal emissyuns, a share set ta rise, thay argue.

T' UNFCCC’s meetins air noteriyus fer taxtual battles an delay, n sum cases prolongyun closure by mer thun a day.

Scyintists air poundyun un ev'r-lowder drumbeet owarnin.

Earth is un track, thay say, fer warmin thishere centry oabout fer degrees Celsius, cursyun future generatyuns wit specees loss, risin seas, drouite, floods an diseese spred.

T' hed ot' UN’s panel oclimate scyintists, Rajendra Pachauri, warnet Thirsdee at onlee about a thousund billyun tonnes ocarbon dioxide (CO2) a'ken still be emittid befor t' targetid acoupla degree limit is breechet.

“We air all un spaceship Earth. We air noe sharyun thishere space oa thousund gigatons oCO2,” he sed.

“We have ta divide up thishere space ekwitablee an n a matt'r ofair an ethical consideratyuns becawz otherwise we air goin ta exceet t' acoupla degrees.”
